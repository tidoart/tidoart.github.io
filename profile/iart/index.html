About

Challenge

Today, hand-painting ability is very important for both of professional and amateur designers.
As a art enthusiast, who has a background in science and engineering, I would like to design and develop an online art education APP based on AI technology.
> How can I learn to paint better?
> Is it possible to teach yourself at home?
> How can I share my learning experiences with others?

Oct - Dec 2018, 8 weeks

Objectives

Design and develop a painting education mobile application based on AI 'BLACK' technology.

Background

Because of rapid development of AI technology, the education filed has also improved in time ,by employing more advance and accurate ways to make both teaching and learning process better.

  • AI market is developing rapidly: It is expected that artificial intelligence in U.S. education will grow by 47.5% from 2017-2021 according to the Artificial Intelligence Market in the US Education Sector report.
  • Online education market has a high demand: There are 182 online education investments in the first half year of 2018, with total financing exceeded 15 billion.
  • Art Training market size: About 40 billion in 2018, and with above 20% annual growth.

  • Human-AI interaction and collaboration
