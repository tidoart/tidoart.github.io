How to learn painting better?


> Creativity and innovation: Skills for the future.

When we look at the world we live in today, it is easy to see how technological advances have completely redefined the way we live and communicate. It is no doubt that technology is able to do more and more things instead of human beings.

In this case, what will be the most coveted skill of the future? The answer should be the capability that computers do not have, that is, creativity.

> The number of students studying creative is rising.

According to HESA statistics for higher education students studied in the UK, the percentage of students undertaking Creative Art and Design has increased by 3% between 2015/16 and 2016/17. Also, among part-time students, there was an increasing number of 19% while undergraduate first year is 39%.




>> Drawing make the ideas visible.

Sketching and drawing is indeed something to express the creative ideas. However, for those who want to learn to draw or improve painting skill, there are usually several constraints:

  • Q1: Difficult to find the right course. There is much information about art schools or classes on the internet, but the students do not even know how to select a suitable one for himself. Choosing the right class to enroll in is sometimes more like a gambling game.

  • Q2: Learning is limited by time and space. It could take students much time between their home or workplace and the campus. Distance challenge is more critical for students who need to work.

  • Q3: Lack of standard evaluation and clear guidance. How to appreciate artwork is a notable issue in the art world. For students, especially those in the beginner stage, it is not easy to judge their skill level objectively and to understand where the difference between the target paintings and themselves is. They cannot get the direction of improvement.

    How might we help art learner form a better education mode?


    > Design and develop a mobile educational app to provide high-quality art courses and intelligent guidance.

    By using AI image processing technology, iart is designed from the perspective of the end users, to provide assistance throughout the whole process of art learning.




    > Establish a comprehensive teaching and learning system to improve the user experience.

    Painting learning is a cyclical and continuous process. iart 'Learning Quality Circle' is a closed-loop structure covering the whole process of teaching-learning-measuring-checking. It would help encourage students to make continuous progress.


    > Develop an adaptive education mode.

    iart is suitable for different kinds of students, such as fine art learners, architectures, designers, and painting enthusiasts. Users can easily choose the lessons according to their actual needs.
