/ The Vision

iart: an AI-based online art education app

To enable students to learn more about painting, we designed a mobile application for art education based on AI technology, to provide a smart, exciting and efficient art learning solution.



Different from other traditional educational institutions, our product is developed based on the state-of-art AI technology, which we have independent intellectual property rights. Considering from both the theoretical and practical and needs, iart offers an innovative solution.



iart consists of three modules:

- Diversified Information Platform

- Personalized Intelligent Teaching System

- Professional art social Network



/ The Requirements

Research and development of AI technology should meet the actual teaching needs.

All of our team members have the background of science and engineering, and we have achieved some recognized results in the field of AI image processing. However, the purpose of the product is to apply the technology to practical applications, instead of pursuing to show the ability of research.

/ The Framework

The decision of the design direction and product positioning.

iart is positioned as an online education app for the art students and based on AI technology. It's an interdisciplinary product combining art education and AI technology, which belongs to the Computer Science branch.



Competitive analysis


There are three main competitors for the product:

1) Traditional art education institutes:
The tradinal art education institutes, such as RCA, xxx, xxx, often have a long history and have a wealth of experience in art education, but most of the courses they offered are off-line.


2) Online or distance learning organization:
With the development of the internet, there are now more and more online education websites, and some of them are very successful and famous, such as Cousera, edX, Khan Academy, etc. But most of the online lessons are targeted to provide basic academic knowledge or actual college/university curriculum course materials. The online experiences related to art is on a relatively small scale. The most common is some artists' website for painting introduction or video playback, such as draw space, Glenn Vilppu, Marc Scheff, etc.


3) Artificial Intelligence (AI) company:
With Google as the representative, some high-tech artificial intelligence companies are engaged in technology development, to explore new technologies or to make new creations. What they are doing may relate to art to some extent, not for art education.



As an art-learning- aid tool, iart is going to link the students' offline painting activities to the online teaching courses. This is a real challenge for both AI technology and art education.
