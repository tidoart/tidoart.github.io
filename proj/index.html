Today, human beings live in an extremely advanced and civilized society. They are constantly asking for higher levels and judging material technology, comfort and functionality. Artists and designers also play an unprecedented role. All life satisfaction depends on the designer's new trends and the new tastes set by the creation.

現在人類生活在極度先進文明的社會，對物質技術、舒適享受與功能上，不斷要求更高水平與評委，藝術家與設計家也同時發揮史無前例的功能，一切生活的滿足，全賴設計家的新創作所掀起的潮流與新品味。
